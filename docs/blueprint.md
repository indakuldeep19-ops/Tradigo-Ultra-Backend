# **App Name**: Tradigo Prime

## Core Features:

- AI Intelligence Hub: Uses generative AI as a tool to synthesize live market data, provide buy/sell signals, and generate instant risk analysis reports for any asset.
- Live Performance Dashboard: High-frequency real-time charts powered by TradingView integration with a sleek, luxury mobile interface.
- Prime Signal Marketplace: A marketplace stored on Firestore where creators can publish and sell premium trading indicators and mentorship packs.
- Trading Reels & Community: A mobile-first short-form video feed for traders to share strategies and market insights with follower engagement.
- Secure Vault Payments: Seamless integration for VIP subscriptions and marketplace transactions via Razorpay and UPI.
- Live Battle Rooms: Firestore-powered public and private rooms for group voice chat and real-time trading competitions.
- AI News Alchemist: A tool that scans global news outlets in real-time to generate condensed, sentiment-analyzed summaries for forex and crypto pairs.

## Style Guidelines:

- A dark, luxury-oriented theme. Primary color is a vibrant 'Ethereal Gold' (#D4AF37) set against a deeply desaturated 'Onyx Black' background (#151412).
- Accent color is a sophisticated 'Burnished Copper' (#B76E59) providing elegant contrast for secondary buttons and indicators.
- A refined pairing of 'Playfair' (serif) for editorial-style luxury headlines and 'Inter' (sans-serif) for high-density trading data and mobile interface clarity.
- Minimalist, thin-stroke gold icons to maintain a premium feel and prevent visual clutter on mobile screens.
- Mobile-first vertical flow with a bottom-tab navigation system optimized for one-handed operation on smartphones.
- Liquid transitions between views and shimmering 'gold leaf' progress bars to emphasize the premium nature of the app.
